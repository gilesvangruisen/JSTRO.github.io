About

Welcome to my Capstone portfolio for the UM Minor in Writing! My name is Joe Ostrow, and I'm a senior concentrating in Biopsychology, Cognition & Neuroscience (BCN) with minors in both Writing and Spanish.

The centerpiece of this portfolio is my Capstone Project, which is an exploration of musical creation, stemming from macro social theory to up-close, personal reasoning.

It also includes an assortment of other writing I have done ranging in subject, genre, length and style. I invite you to explore as little or as much as you wish!
